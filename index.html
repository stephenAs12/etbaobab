ABOUT US




etbaobab was founded with a vision to share the extraordinary benefits of the baobab tree with the world. Based in Ethiopia, we are proud to work directly with local farmers, ensuring the highest quality products while promoting fair trade and sustainable practices. Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the baobab tree—a symbol of resilience and life in arid regions. Today, we are committed to harnessing its potential to improve health, support livelihoods, and protect the environment.

Our Mission: To deliver premium Ethiopian baobab products while empowering local communities and fostering global awareness about the benefits of this incredible tree.

Our Vision: A world where the baobab’s potential is fully realized, contributing to a healthier planet and prosperous communities. With every product you purchase, you join us in our mission to create positive change—one baobab at a time.
